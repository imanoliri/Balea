Each bottle of Balea costs about €5 to make — and hold a full liter, not 75cl like most wines.

If we sold each bottle for €7 — making a €2 margin — we would need to sell around 30,000 liters per year just to support a single worker earning €2,500 net per month.

If you believe in fairness and open brewing, consider donating to cover the cost of this one.
